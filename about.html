About Us

Surron™ is an electric bike manufacturer founded in 2014. A combination of innovation, industry-leading specialists, cutting-edge technology, and meticulous product development led to the release of the original "e-dirt bike," the Light Bee, in 2018. The Light Bee has since become loved around the globe; Surron™ has sold more than 70,000 Light Bee units worldwide.
